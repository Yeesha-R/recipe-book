# Crepes Ingredients

## Main Batter
- 1 cup (125g) all-purpose flour
- 2 large eggs
- 1.5 cups (360ml) milk
- 2 tablespoons (28g) unsalted butter, melted
- 1 tablespoon granulated sugar (omit for savory crepes)
- .5 teaspoon salt
- .5 teaspoon vanilla extract (for sweet crepes)

## Cooking
- 2-3 tablespoons butter for cooking

## Optional Fillings
### Sweet Options
- Nutella & banana slices
- Fresh berries & whipped cream
- Lemon juice & powdered sugar
- Maple syrup & butter

### Savory Options
- Ham & cheese
- Spinach & feta
- Mushrooms & cream sauce
- Smoked salmon & creme fraiche
