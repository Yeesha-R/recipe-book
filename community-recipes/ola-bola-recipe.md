A classic Eggs Benedict consists of a toasted English muffin half topped with Canadian bacon, a poached egg, and hollandaise sauce, garnished with chives or paprika. 
Ingredients
For the Eggs Benedict:
4 English muffins, split
8 slices Canadian bacon (back bacon)
8 large eggs
1 Tbsp white vinegar (for poaching water)
Butter, for toasting muffins
Fresh chives or paprika, for garnish
For the Hollandaise Sauce:
1/2 cup unsalted butter (10 Tbsp), melted
3 large egg yolks
2 Tbsp fresh lemon juice
1/4 tsp salt
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper (or white pepper)
